## f87r - Opening Pharmaceutical Recipes

### Croatian Reading
```
**Kostedi** hol ar **dain** šedi **kostal**
Šol **dar** **kostain** hedi **ol** **kostedi**
**Dain** ar šor **kostal** hol šedi

Otrar **dain** hedi ar ostain šol
**Kostedi** **ol** šor **kostain** **dar** al
Hol **dain** **kostain** ar šedi **kostedi** **ol** **dar**
```

### Interpretive Translation
```
Bone preparation: combine oil, water, dose, soaked root, bone-vessel.
Oil infusion: portion, bones, cooked root, oil, bone preparation.
Dose: water, soak-liquid, bone-vessel, combined oil, soaked root.

Vessel-process: dose, cooked root, water, vessels, oil infusion.
Bone preparation: oil, soak-liquid, bones, portion, container.
Combined oil: dose, bones, water, soaked root, bone preparation, oil, portion.
```

### Recipe Summary
**Bone-Calcium Preparation**
1. Combine bone material with oil and water
2. Add soaked root to bone vessel
3. Create oil infusion with bone portions
4. Process cooked roots in vessels
5. Final combination of all elements


### Medical Context

These recipes describe the preparation of bone-derived medicines, consistent with:
- Medieval calcium supplements
- Bone char preparations
- Calcinated bone remedies
- Marrow extractions

The repeated pattern of oil + water + bone + root suggests standardized pharmaceutical procedures for extracting and preserving bone-derived medicinal compounds.
